ABOUT

AMSTERDAM TREES

This app is generating a soundscape using data from the trees around you. Walk around and enjoy an intense relationship with the great green giants of beautiful Amsterdam through a continuously changing melody!

This app can also be used to get info about the trees by touching the circles on the map.

KNOOIWARK

Knooiwark is a one-person indie production studio by Geert Dijkstra into exploring new ways of making music.

If you like thise app, please consider a small donation or listen to the music of Knooiwark on Spotify
